Step 2 — Terrain roughness correction (gradient wind two-way trip):
Up:   V600m = V10m,site × (600/10)α_site
Down: V10m,open = V600m × (10/600)0.14
Combined: V10m,open = V10m,site × 60(α_site − 0.14)
60 = 600m gradient height ÷ 10m reference. α_open=0.14 (OrbitalStack standard)

* Radiation assumption: EPW stores radiation as Wh/m² (hourly accumulated irradiance per unit area). TC360 expects W (watts = power). For hourly data, 1 Wh/m² over 1 hour equals 1 W·h/m², and assuming a unit reference area of 1 m², the numeric value is preserved as W. This is standard meteorological practice (power per unit area proxy). If your simulation requires actual surface area, multiply the output values by the true area in m².
